Sponsorship & Exhibition Opportunities

Partner with ICUMSAT 2025 to showcase your organization to leading experts, researchers, and industry professionals in ultrasonics and advanced materials. We offer a range of sponsorship tiers and exhibition packages tailored to maximize your visibility.

Platinum Sponsor

  • Prominent logo placement across event materials and website
  • Prime exhibition booth location

  • Speaking opportunity in a plenary session

Gold Sponsor

  • Logo featured on conference website and signage

Silver Sponsor
