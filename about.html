About ICUMSAT 2025

The International Conference on Ultrasonics (ICUMSAT 2025) will be held in Chennai, India from January 21-23, 2026. This premier conference brings together researchers, scientists, engineers, and practitioners to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of Ultrasonics and its applications.

Conference Themes

  • Ultrasonic Imaging and Diagnostics
  • Industrial Applications of Ultrasonics
  • Medical Ultrasonics
  • Ultrasonic Materials Characterization
  • Ultrasonic Transducers and Arrays
  • Ultrasonic Motors and Actuators
  • Nonlinear Ultrasonics
  • Ultrasonic Measurement Techniques
  • Ultrasonic NDT/NDE
  • Acoustic Microscopy
  • Ultrasonic MEMS
  • Ultrasonic Cleaning and Processing

About Chennai

Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal, it is one of the largest cultural, economic and educational centers of south India. Chennai is home to many educational and research institutions and has a rich cultural heritage.

Venue

The conference will be held at Easwari Engineering College, a premier educational institution in Chennai known for its excellence in engineering education and research.
